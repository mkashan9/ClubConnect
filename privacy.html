Privacy Policy

How ClubConnect collects, uses and protects information for clubs, coaches and players using our platform.

Last updated: 20 February 2025  •  Applies to all ClubConnect websites and dashboards.

1. Introduction

ClubConnect is designed to help clubs, coaches and players manage sports operations in a simple and secure way. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in three main ways:

  • Information you provide – such as your name, email address, password, club details and profile information you enter into the platform.
  • Usage data – such as pages visited, features used, approximate location (from IP address), device type and browser.
  • Content added by your club – such as fixtures, performance stats, attendance, messages and announcements.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the ClubConnect platform and features.
  • Create and manage user accounts and club profiles.
  • Send important service emails (e.g. security alerts, updates).
  • Support clubs in managing squads, payments, schedules and communication.
  • Analyse how the platform is used so we can improve performance and user experience.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds, depending on your location:

  • To perform our contract with you or your club.
  • With your consent (for example, marketing emails).
  • For our legitimate interests in running and improving the service.
  • To comply with legal obligations where required.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Your club – information is visible to club owners and coaches according to their role in the platform.
  • Service providers – such as hosting, analytics or email providers that help us operate ClubConnect.
  • Authorities – if required by law, court order or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as your account or club remains active, and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal, accounting or reporting requirements. Clubs may also retain historical performance and schedule data for their own records.

7. Security

We use technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS), access controls and regular monitoring. No system is completely secure, so we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your ClubConnect account.

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences and understand how the platform is used. For more detail, please see our Cookie Policy.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as:

  • Accessing the personal data we hold about you.
  • Requesting corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Asking for deletion of certain data, subject to legal requirements.
  • Opting out of marketing communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link or contacting us.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us via the form on our contact page.

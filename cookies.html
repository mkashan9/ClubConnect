Cookie Policy

How and why ClubConnect uses cookies and similar technologies on our websites and dashboards.

Last updated: 20 February 2025 • This Cookie Policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site recognise your device, remember preferences and understand how the service is being used.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly necessary cookies – required for basic functionality such as logging in, keeping you signed in and routing requests securely.
  • Preference cookies – remember language, theme and basic settings so your experience is consistent.
  • Analytics cookies – help us understand how users navigate the platform so we can improve performance and usability.
  • Functional cookies – support features such as interactive elements, notifications or third-party integrations.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services (for example, analytics or error monitoring tools) that set their own cookies to help us measure how ClubConnect is used. These providers are carefully selected and are only allowed to use data for purposes we define.

4. How You Can Control Cookies

You have several options to control or limit cookies:

  • Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings pages.
  • You can usually choose to block cookies from specific websites or types of cookies (for example, third-party cookies).
  • If you block essential cookies, some ClubConnect features may not work correctly.

5. Do We Use Similar Technologies?

In addition to cookies, we may use technologies such as local storage or session storage in your browser to remember state, preferences and cached data. These are used in a similar way to cookies but are stored slightly differently on your device.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy over time to reflect new features, legal requirements or improvements. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about how we use cookies, please contact us via the form on our contact page.
